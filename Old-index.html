About Nigel Moxon

Excel Expert with 25+ Years Accounting Experience

I'm Nigel Moxon, founder of Moxon Training & Consultancy. With over 25 years of experience working as an accountant at all levels, I've seen firsthand how Excel can transform business operations when used effectively.

My mission is to help businesses and professionals move beyond basic spreadsheet use to unlock the full power of Excel for data analysis, reporting, and decision-making.

Unlike generic training courses, I focus on the practical skills you actually need in your day-to-day work. My approach is hands-on, results-oriented, and tailored to your specific business needs.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to enhance your existing skills, I can help you work smarter with Excel and get more value from your business data.
